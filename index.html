Feature Section

BREATHABLE
AND DURABLE

While the third generation sacrificed durability with lightheartedness, the Romales 4 finds the happy marriage between both sides. Blending the classic Flyknit with thick cotton mesh for the ankle for durability, and reinforcing the durability of toe box mesh.

TRUE TO
FIT TOE BOX

Athletes toes shape. A narrower space will help consolidating the contact and force to the ground, minimizing heel and toe shift during the lift.


TPU SOLE

Reinforced rigidness in midsole, creating strong base for heavy lifts. Updated outsole shape for steady contact when landing a lift.

DUAL STRAP SYSTEM

Brought back the classic dual straps system; the system providing different levels of tightness and security to fit the different needs of the lifters, as well as different shapes of their foot, ensuring constant contact with the ground, the shoes, and the feet.
‍

CLASSIC HEEL HEIGHT

Keeping the heel height at 20mm(.75”), perfected from the evolution of the sport of weightlifting itself, reserving the preciseness and effectiveness.
